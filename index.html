WHAT IS

TAX?


Tax is money that you pay to the government. It’s used to fund public services such as education, healthcare and roads.  Tax is important because it helps to pay for the public services that we all rely on. Without tax, we would not have access to good schools, hospitals and other essential services.  Most people pay tax and the amount of tax you have to pay depends on your income, your spending and your circumstances.
